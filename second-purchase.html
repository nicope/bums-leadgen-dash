Key Findings & Optimisation Actions
📧 Email Flow
✅ Welcome email drives majority of revenue
The welcome message achieves strong open and click rates, generating significant RPR. This first touchpoint is critical for 2nd purchase intent.
💡 Care Guide shows healthy engagement
The care/content message maintains decent open and click rates, building brand affinity alongside purchase intent.
💡 Reward messages see variable performance
Reward offers for both member and non-member segments show differing engagement levels. Consider segment-specific offers.
⚠️ Discount fatigue in later emails
The 25% Off emails (especially later ones) show declining performance. May need to rotate offer types or increase urgency.
💬 SMS Flow
✅ SMS drives meaningful revenue
The SMS flow, while smaller in volume than email, shows strong per-message performance and good overall RPR.
💡 25% Off SMS shows strong engagement
The discount SMS message achieves solid click and conversion rates, making it a valuable part of the sequence.
💡 Rewards SMS has lower engagement
The rewards offer SMS shows modest performance. Consider timing, offer structure, or message copy optimization.
⚠️ Monitor unsubscribe rates
SMS unsubscribe rates should be monitored closely. Ensure frequency and relevance are balanced to maintain list health.
