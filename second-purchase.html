Key Insights & Actions
✓ Welcome VIP Email Drives 53% of Total Flow Revenue
The first message in the sequence, "Welcome VIP" (80.6% open rate, 5.46% CTR), drives $4,919.68 of the $8,883.43 email revenue. This is the highest-performing message. Consider:
• A/B test alternate hero images or subject lines to further boost performance
• Ensure this message is delivered immediately post-purchase to maximize engagement
✓ Baby Segment Outperforms: 53.5% Open Rate (Rewards Email), Highest CVR
The Baby segment "Rewards — Member" email shows 53.5% open rate with 0.103% CVR (highest single message CVR). Baby segment also dominates 25% Off campaigns:
• Baby #1: 48.4% open, 1.99% CTR (highest overall CTR)
• Baby segment converts 2-3x better than Other/Kid segments on discounted offers
⚠ Care Guide Message Underperforms: 67.4% Open, 0.59% CTR
The second message "Care Guide" has a significant drop-off in CTR (0.59%) and only generates $919.68 in revenue. Possible actions:
• Test alternative copy focused on product benefits or VIP tier perks
• Move this earlier in the sequence or consider replacing with product recommendations
• Current content may not drive purchase intent; educational content works better in nurture flows
⚠ SMS Underperforming vs. Welcome Series: RPR $0.046 vs $0.25+
SMS channel delivers only $385.03 revenue on 8,351 recipients (RPR: $0.046), significantly lower than Welcome Series SMS (~$0.25 RPR). Despite decent click rates (4.89% blended):
• Consider adding incentive/urgency language to drive clicks to conversions
• Test timing variation (different days/times for Rewards vs 25% Off SMS)
• Evaluate SMS segment — may only be reaching lower-intent customers
→ Rewards Email Segment Split: Members vs Non-Members
Two messages split by membership status. Key difference:
• Members (3,909 recipients): 53.5% open, 0.103% CVR, $0.059 RPR
• Non-Members (11,321 recipients): 52.8% open, 0.053% CVR, $0.042 RPR
• Members convert at 2x the rate — consider more aggressive VIP benefits messaging to drive enrollment
→ Toddler Segment Shows Strong Performance on 25% Off
Among age-segmented 25% Off emails, Toddler segment delivers:
• Toddler #2: $424.57 revenue, 0.122% CVR (tied for highest in segment offers)
• Toddler #1: 51.7% open rate, 1.45% CTR
• Consider expanding product selection or offer depth for Toddler buyers
